Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on earning organic traffic through unpaid listings by optimizing your site's content and technical structure over time. Google Ads (PPC) is a paid strategy where you pay for immediate visibility and clicks at the top of search results. Both are effective, but they serve different timelines and goals.
How long does it take to see results from SEO?
SEO is a long-term investment. While some technical fixes can show immediate improvements, significant organic ranking growth typically takes 3 to 6 months. This depends on your industry's competitiveness, your current website authority, and the consistency of the strategy implemented.
Do you work with businesses outside of Vancouver, WA?
Yes! While our roots and specialty are in Vancouver and the Pacific Northwest, our digital strategies are effective anywhere. We work with clients across the country, tailoring our approach to their specific local markets and customer behaviors.
How do you measure the success of a marketing campaign?
We focus on metrics that matter to your bottom line: Leads (calls, form submissions), Cost Per Lead (CPL), and Return on Ad Spend (ROAS). We provide transparent, regular reporting that moves beyond vanity metrics like "impressions" to show you actual business growth.
Why is a website audit important?
A website audit reveals the hidden issues preventing your site from ranking high or converting visitors. It checks for broken links, slow load speeds, mobile responsiveness issues, and SEO gaps. It's the first step in building a roadmap for digital success.
