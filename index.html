Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results from SEO?
SEO is a long-term strategy. While some technical fixes can show immediate improvements, significant organic growth typically takes 3 to 6 months. This depends on your industry's competitiveness and your website's current history.
What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads?
Google Ads (PPC) puts you at the top of search results instantly for a fee per click. SEO involves optimizing your site to rank organically (for free) over time. We often recommend a hybrid strategy for the best results.
Why is Local SEO important for my business?
If you serve a specific geographic area like Vancouver, Local SEO ensures you appear in the "Map Pack" and local searches. This is crucial because nearly 50% of all Google searches have local intent.
Do you provide monthly performance reports?
Absolutely. We believe in total transparency. You will receive detailed monthly reports covering traffic, rankings, leads generated, and actionable insights for the next month's strategy.
Are there long-term contracts?
We work to earn your business every month. While we recommend committing to at least 6 months for SEO to see real ROI, we offer flexible terms tailored to your business needs.
